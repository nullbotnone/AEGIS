Worst-case probe overhead measured by repeatedly exercising a single syscall family with the AEGIS eBPF probe attached. File-centric syscalls incur the highest overhead: \texttt{openat} increases elapsed time by 23.3\% and \texttt{read} by 28.2\%, whereas network connection monitoring adds only 5.35\% for \texttt{connect}. These microbenchmarks bound the probe cost in isolation and are intentionally more pessimistic than the end-to-end workload measurements.
