Figure 8: Performance Overhead Analysis. Overhead percentage (bars) and attestation latency (line) as a function of attestation interval (§5.3). At 100ms intervals, overhead is 8.0% with 50ms latency. At the recommended 1s interval, overhead drops to 1.5% with 500ms latency. At 10s intervals, overhead is negligible (0.2%) but latency reaches 5 seconds. The 1-second interval offers the best balance between detection timeliness and performance impact.
