Detection outcomes and mean analysis time for five defenses evaluated on the same attack traces. Network DLP, filesystem auditing, and strict sandboxing each detect only two of four attacks (50\%), while per-agent analytics misses all four. AEGIS is the only mechanism that detects all attack classes (100\%) and still completes its analysis in 0.081\,ms on average, compared with 0.005--0.010\,ms for the simpler baselines.
