Simulation-based scaling study for the paper's appendix. Overhead remains below 5\% in 14 of 16 tested configurations. For the mixed workload, overhead drops from 1.92\% at a 100\,ms interval to 1.17\% at 1\,s; at a fixed 1\,s interval, overhead is 1.17\% for 10 agents, 2.34\% for 50 agents, 2.12\% for 100 agents, and 3.11\% for 500 agents. Workload sensitivity is dominated by I/O intensity, ranging from 0.30\% for compute-heavy jobs to 3.94\% for I/O-heavy jobs.
