Simulation-based scaling study for the paper's appendix. Overhead remains below 5\% in 11 of 16 tested configurations. For the mixed workload, overhead drops from 3.61\% at a 100\,ms interval to 2.14\% at 1\,s; at a fixed 1\,s interval, overhead is 1.88\% for 10 agents, 3.67\% for 50 agents, 9.38\% for 100 agents, and 25.36\% for 500 agents. Workload sensitivity is dominated by I/O intensity, ranging from 0.63\% for compute-heavy jobs to 8.87\% for I/O-heavy jobs.
