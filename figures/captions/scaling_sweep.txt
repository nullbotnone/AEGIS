Simulation-based scaling study for the paper. Overhead remains below 5\% in 14 of 16 tested configurations. In the mixed-workload interval sweep at 10 agents, overhead is 1.60\% at 100\,ms, 1.13\% at 1\,s, and 1.05\% at 10\,s. At a fixed 1\,s interval, mixed-workload overhead is 1.10\% for 10 agents, 2.43\% for 50 agents, 3.32\% for 100 agents, and 4.01\% for 500 agents. Workload sensitivity ranges from 0.33\% for compute-heavy jobs to 3.87\% for I/O-heavy jobs.
