Controlled simulated ablation isolating the four specialized detection mechanisms. Full AEGIS detects all four attack classes, and removing any one mechanism drops overall detection to 75\% by allowing exactly the matching attack class to evade detection: no volume limits misses the volume attack, no sensitive-file detection misses the sensitive-file attack, no covert-channel logic misses the covert-channel attack, and no injection signatures misses the supply-chain attack. A minimal path-only policy detects none of the attacks.
