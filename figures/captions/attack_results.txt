Figure 7: Attack Experiment Results. Summary of four attack experiments (§5.1) showing bytes exfiltrated (bars) and detection events triggered (line). Basic attacks (Exp 1: filesystem injection, Exp 2: co-location) exfiltrated 68 and 50 bytes respectively, each triggering 1 detection. Advanced attacks (Exp 3: supply chain, Exp 4: coordinated) exfiltrated 519 and 521 bytes, triggering 7 detections each as multiple constraint violations were observed across different dimensions.
